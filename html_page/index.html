Tornadoes In the United State In 2021

The report demonstrates the situation of tornadoes by Width, Degree of Severity, and Time.

Map of Width and Magnitude of Tornadoes

The level of tonadoes Magnitude is demonstrated by the size of circle, the length of the width is demonstrated by by the

sequential from light to grey. We can observe that most of the tornadoes happened in the midwest and south of the United State.

Also as we can observe that most of the Magnitude of Tornadoes is postively corresponded to width of tornadoes,

the larger the circle the darker the color in general.

We can see the tornaodes in the south has deeper magnitude and width compare to other area.

Scatterplot of len and width With Regions And Different Period Of A Day

The states in the United State insubdivided by bureau region in the left hand side. Using direct selection filter

on legend,we can filter each region with scatterplot.The states in the United State in the right hand side is

color hue by period of a day.Using direct selection filter on legend, we can filter each time period with scatterplot.

We can observe that len and wid are positive correlated. If an event of tornadoes has higher len it usually have higher wid.

By clicking the legend, we will notice that the extreme high value of len and wid usually locate at the south and midwest.

Also the extreme high value of len and wid usually happens at afternoon and evening

The Records of Tornadoes Vary Around Period of A Day

We can see the frequency of tornadoes relates to period of a day with observing the stacked bar chart and piechart with different months below.

May and December are the peak that contain the two highest records of tornadoes. In total the records happened in evening

and after take up 70% of total records in the US during 2021.

Most of the tornadoes event happened in afternoon and evening .

The Records of Tornadoes Vary Around The Regions

We can see the frequency of tornadoes relates region with observing the stacked bar chart and piechart with different months below.

In total the records located in the South and Midwest after take up 80% of total records in the US during 2021.

Most of the tornadoes event happened in the region of midwest and south .
